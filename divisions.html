The Think Tank

A Vision Born From Courage & Craft

In a world dominated by nine-to-five routines and familiar paths, Naved Akram stands out as a beacon of courage and inspiration. A Civil Engineer armed with an MBA in Construction Management, he built a dynamic career spanning projects across India, Saudi Arabia, and Dubai.

Embracing his entrepreneurial spirit, Naved returned to Amravati and founded NVD Group — three specialized companies united by a shared commitment to quality, innovation, and lasting client relationships. Like a master architect, he crafted each venture with passion and precision, weaving together creativity, innovation, and business acumen.

Today, NVD Group is a growing collective of problem solvers, creative thinkers, and solution providers — offering a complete project lifecycle service from feasibility study to finishing touch.

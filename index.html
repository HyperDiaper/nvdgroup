The Think Tank

A Vision Born From Courage & Craft

Founded by Naved Akram — a Civil Engineer with an MBA in Construction Management — NVD Group was born from a deliberate step away from corporate certainty and toward entrepreneurial purpose.

After leaving his mark on projects across India, Saudi Arabia, and Dubai, Naved returned home to Amravati and founded NVD Group: three specialized companies united by a shared commitment to quality, innovation, and lasting client relationships.

Today, NVD Group is a growing collective of problem solvers, creative thinkers, and solution providers — offering a complete project lifecycle service from feasibility study to finishing touch.
